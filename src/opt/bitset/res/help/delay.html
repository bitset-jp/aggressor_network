delay - Delay Packet Delay the outgoing packets to the selected network interface. Delay tim, jitter time and the fluctuation rate of jitter time can be set. USAGE: delay TIME (JITTER) (CORRELATION) ARGUMENTS: TIMEdelay time JITTERjitter time CORRELATIONfluctuation rate (%) Time Unit - ssecond - msmillisecond - usmicrosecond EXAMPLES: 0 > delay 100ms... set delay 0*> update...
